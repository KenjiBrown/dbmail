DBMAIL-SIEVED(8)
================

NAME
----
dbmail-sieved - The Manage Sieve daemon, used for managing Sieve scripts for
a user's email account.

SYNOPSIS
--------
....
dbmail-sieved [common options]
dbmail-sieved [common options] --no-daemon
dbmail-sieved [common options] --inetd
....

DESCRIPTION
-----------
The dbmail-sieved process listens, by default, on port 2000
of the localhost interface.
